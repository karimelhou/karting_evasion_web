## Highlights

- Nine turns including a reverse hairpin plus a unique bridge-underpass combo.
- LiveTiming displayed on panoramic screens throughout the hall.
- Perfect for coaching sessions, training camps and winter events.

### Driving tips

Maintain momentum through the double chicane and brake late to launch out of the bridge section.
